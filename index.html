Bike Sales Analysis

The Bike Sales dataset spans 6 years (2011-2016) and includes data from 8 countries: Australia, Canada, France, Germany, the United Kingdom, and the United States. It tracks three sales categories: bikes, bike accessories, and bike-related clothing. Within the bike category, there are three types of bikes. The data is segmented by gender (male and female) and age groups: youth, young adults, adults, and seniors. This comprehensive dataset allows for detailed analysis of sales trends and demographic preferences across different regions and product categories.
